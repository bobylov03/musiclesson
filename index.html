Meet Your Teacher: Anna Bobylyova

Anna Bobylyova is a passionate and highly qualified music educator with over two decades of musical excellence. Originally from Ukraine, she began her musical journey at the age of 5, progressing through specialized music schools and graduating with honors from a professional music college.

She holds distinguished Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Music Performance and Education, which provide a solid foundation for her innovative teaching methodology.

As a performer, Anna has graced stages across Europe, the UAE, India, and worldwide during contracts with Princess Cruises. This international experience enriches her teaching with cultural depth and professional insight.

Since relocating to Canada, Anna has taught at the Mississauga Fine Arts Academy and Piccolo Music Academy in North York, receiving outstanding feedback from students and parents. Her students—many of whom start as absolute beginners—often gain the confidence to perform on stage within just six months.

I believe performing is a vital part of music education. It builds self-confidence, focus, and emotional growth. Seeing a child smile under the applause is priceless. My goal is to help each student enjoy music, feel proud of their progress, and develop a lifelong love for learning and creativity.

Our Performance Philosophy

Build Confidence Through Performance

At Shine On Stage, we believe regular performance opportunities are essential for musical growth. Students showcase their progress through:

  • Bi-annual recitals for family and friends
  • Community events throughout Muskoka
  • Festival preparations and competitions
  • Informal gatherings с fellow students

These experiences help students overcome stage fright, build self-esteem, and develop poise that extends beyond music into all areas of life.

Whether your goal is professional musicianship or personal enjoyment, learning to shine on stage is a skill that lasts a lifetime.
